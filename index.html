About the Author

Having graduated from Madras Christian College, Chennai in 1972 in Science, G.V. Kumar joined India Meteorological Department, Regional Meteorological Centre, Chennai on 31 December 1974. He served the department for nearly 37 years and retired from service on 31 January 2012 as Meteorologist B from RMC Chennai.

He has rich experience in inspection, installation and calibration of meteorological instruments including establishment of meteorological observatories.
